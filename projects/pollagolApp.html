This personal application developed to play with friends guessing results and possible events in soccer matches. The user can create his account, join a tournament, enter his predictions for the previously created matches and see his score and results of other users in real time.

In addition, the application has a control panel that allows the tournament administrator (Game Master) to create and customize the rules of the game, matches, tournament and kick users.
