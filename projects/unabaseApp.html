This application allows a creative world company to have total control of its projects from its creation, generate the project budget, convert it into a business and invoice it. This allows companies to have full control of their finances. In addition, the application has a network section that allows accounts to share projects with collaborators.

I was hired to work on the Front-End development of this application by the unabase company using the Vue js framework. 3 months later I was promoted to full-stack developer. Among the tasks of the position I can mention, continue with the Front-End work, develop the entire login environment for new users. In addition to creating a development pipeline in Heroku.

I am currently Project Manager of the project in which I must maintain the development of the project based on the company's requirements.
